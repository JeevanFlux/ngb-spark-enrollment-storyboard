SPARK · NEXT GEN BENEFITS · STORYBOARD

From "Enroll Now" to a conversation that fits Nicole's life.

Nicole is a new hire at Acme Corp. She has 57 days to enroll in benefits. Instead of a 6-step form wizard, Spark walks her through one continuous conversation — confirming what's on file, adding her new daughter Maya, gathering health and money context, and producing a personalized starter plan. Below: 6 main-path frames, plus 2 edge-case branches that appear when something needs to be edited or escalated.

If Nicole needs to edit a field — these patterns kick in

These two frames aren't on the main story above — they're alternate paths from Frame 3 (Profile review). They handle the cases where Nicole spots an error in her file. Both stay inside the conversation surface; neither takes her to a separate page.

BRANCH · LOCKED FIELD

Spark drafts the HR email for her

Nicole says her last name needs updating (just got married). Last name is locked — Acme HR controls it. Instead of a dead-end "contact HR" message, Spark drafts a complete email request, prefilled with everything HR needs. Nicole reviews, edits if needed, hits Send. The file panel tags the field "✉ HR request drafted." Enrollment continues — doesn't have to wait.
