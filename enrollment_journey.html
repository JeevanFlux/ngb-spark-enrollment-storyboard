WEXLEY · NEXT GEN BENEFITS · STORYBOARD

From "Enroll Now" to a conversation that fits Nicole's life.

Nicole is a new hire at Acme Corp with a spouse and a 2-year-old. She has 57 days to enroll. Instead of a 6-step form wizard, Wexley walks her through one continuous conversation — confirming what's on file, adding her daughter Maya, surfacing the closest-match plan with the trade-offs laid out, simulating real-life scenarios, and ultimately saving her family ~$2,100 in taxes. We educate; we don't recommend. The choice stays hers. Below: 26 frames covering all three phases of enrollment.

End of storyboard · 26 frames, 3 phases, ~14 minutes

That's the complete reimagined enrollment experience for Nicole. Phase 1 got Wexley up to speed on her family. Phase 2 walked through health plans (with the spousal trade-off), sheltered $7,000 of pre-tax money, presented supplemental neutrally, and surfaced curated partner benefits with paycheck deduction. Phase 3 got it signed and confirmed with a clear timeline of what comes next.

All of this happened on one continuous chat surface, not separate pages — these freeze-frames represent moments in time within that single conversation. The voice-mode state (Frame 1b) shows what hands-free input looks like for the mobile-first majority. We educate; we don't recommend. The choice always stays Nicole's.
