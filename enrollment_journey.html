SPARK · NEXT GEN BENEFITS · STORYBOARD

From "Enroll Now" to a conversation that fits Nicole's life.

Nicole is a new hire at Acme Corp with a spouse and a 2-year-old. She has 57 days to enroll. Instead of a 6-step form wizard, Spark walks her through one continuous conversation — confirming what's on file, adding her daughter Maya, recommending plans personalized to her family, simulating real-life scenarios, and ultimately saving her ~$2,100 in taxes. Below: 22 frames covering all three phases of enrollment.

End of storyboard · 22 frames, 3 phases, 14 minutes

That's the complete reimagined enrollment experience for Nicole. Phase 1 got Spark up to speed on her family. Phase 2 recommended health plans, sheltered $7,000 of pre-tax money, and skipped what didn't apply. Phase 3 got it signed and confirmed with a clear timeline of what comes next.

All of this happened on one continuous chat surface, not separate pages — these freeze-frames represent moments in time within that single conversation.
