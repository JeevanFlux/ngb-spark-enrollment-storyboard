WEXLEY · NEXT GEN BENEFITS · STORYBOARD

From "Enroll Now" to a conversation that fits Nicole's life.

A reimagined benefits enrollment experience built around Wexley, a conversational AI assistant. Nicole — a new hire at Acme Corp with a spouse and a 2-year-old — moves through enrollment as a single continuous conversation instead of a 6-step wizard. Wexley gathers context, surfaces the closest-match plan with the trade-offs laid out, simulates real-life scenarios, and saves her family ~$2,100 in taxes — all while keeping every option transparent and every decision hers. We educate; we don't recommend.
