Alternative

💡
The math: Premium costs $169/year more than Basic. The break-even is one filling + one crown — about $400 in major work — and you've saved more than the upgrade cost. For a family with a toddler, that's a "when, not if" calculation. That said, Basic is a real option if you'd rather pay less now and switch up later.
